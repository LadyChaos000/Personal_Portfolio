La Elite Floral App Case Study

01. Project Overview

Introduction


La Elite Florist is dedicated to enriching consumers' lives by offering a diverse selection of flowers tailored to individual preferences and needs. Its extensive range of thoughtfully curated blooms enhances various occasions and brings joy to personal spaces. The clientele includes two primary groups: those seeking guidance in floral arrangements and busy professionals closely connected with floral design. This project was completed as part of the Google UX Design Professional Certificate, demonstrating a commitment to creating user-centric designs that meet real-world needs.

02. Problem Statement

“Customers struggle with selecting the right floral arrangements for specific occasions due to a lack of expertise. Additionally, busy schedules make it challenging to place orders for sizable floral arrangements on short notice.”

02. Design Process


The design process for the La Elite Florist app began with thorough user research and competetive audits, focusing on understanding the needs of two primary user groups: busy professionals and those seeking floral arrangement guidance. Through interviews and empathy maps, I identified pain points such as time constraints and the lack of an easy-to-use platform for selecting floral arrangements. Based on these insights, I created personas and user journey maps, guiding the design process toward addressing key challenges like accessibility and ease of navigation.

Next, I moved on to creating paper wireframes, exploring various layout possibilities to ensure a seamless preview experience. These wireframes were then translated into digital versions, where I refined the design further by incorporating user feedback. Usability studies played a pivotal role in this stage, revealing critical improvements such as simplifying navigation and enhancing text readability. Iterative testing and feedback helped shape the app into a more intuitive and accessible platform for users.

The final high-fidelity prototype showcased a polished user interface. By prioritizing both functionality and accessibility, the app successfully enabled users to preview floral arrangements and place orders efficiently, while creating a more enjoyable and inclusive user experience.
