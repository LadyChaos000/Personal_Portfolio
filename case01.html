C3S Portal Case Study

01. Project Overview

Introduction


The C3S project, a collaborative effort to enhance supply chain management for Heritage Lanka Pvt Ltd, represents a significant advancement in integrating technology with operational efficiency. This project focused on developing an innovative web application tailored to streamline and optimize various facets of supply chain processes.

The primary objective of the C3S project was to address the complexities and challenges faced by Heritage Lanka, a leading coconut and natural products manufacturer and exporter in Sri Lanka. By leveraging cutting-edge technologies and user-centric design principles, the project aimed to deliver a solution that would improve real-time inventory tracking, supplier performance monitoring, and overall order management.

02. Problem Statement

“Heritage Lanka experiences frequent stockouts and communication breakdowns with suppliers when they try to manage their supply chain operations using outdated and disconnected systems. This is a problem because it leads to production delays, order inaccuracies, and increased operational costs, negatively impacting both the user experience and the company's ability to meet customer demand and maintain competitiveness.”

02. Design Process


The C3S Project first started with an onboarding session with the NSBM project team, who reached out to me to craft the UI/UX design for their project concept. Guided by the team's research on Heritage Lanka's supply chain management, I identified key gaps in the existing systems and explored innovative features to be integrated into the C3S project. These insights laid the foundation for the creation of low-fidelity wireframes, which were instrumental in gathering feedback on the user experience and initial design concepts. During this phase, I employed site maps and user personas to shape the design.

In collaboration with Heritage Lanka and the project team, I refined the wireframes, progressing to the development of high-fidelity wireframes. These included detailed design elements such as imagery, color schemes, and custom content that resonated with the company's branding and user requirements. This stage was crucial for aligning the design with the company's vision and ensuring that all visual and functional aspects were meticulously crafted to enhance the user experience.

Upon approval of the high-fidelity wireframes, I facilitated the seamless handoff to the project team, ensuring that all design assets and specifications were communicated effectively. Additionally, I provided guidance and conducted usability testing to confirm that the final web application design met the stringent design standards. This thorough approach ensured that the design effectively addressed Heritage Lanka's supply chain challenges and enhanced overall operational efficiency.
